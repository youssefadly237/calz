# Gregorian Calendar

## Overview

- Civil calendar
- Epoch: 0001-01-01
- Variants: Proleptic, Cutover
- Day begins midnight

## Leap Year Rules

- Divisible by 4 → leap year
- Divisible by 100 → not leap year unless divisible by 400
- Examples:
  - 2000 → leap
  - 1800 → not leap
  - 2004 → leap
